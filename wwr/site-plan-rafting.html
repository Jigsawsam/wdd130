Overview

Purpose

At our rafting business, our purpose is to provide our customers with an exhilarating outdoor adventure that immerses them in the natural beauty of our area. Our aim is to offer a fun and safe experience that connects people with nature and brings them together for an unforgettable adventure. Join us and let us help you create memories that will last a lifetime.

Audience

Our water rafting website is designed for adventure seekers who are looking for a thrilling and unforgettable experience in the great outdoors. Our target audience is primarily made up of individuals and groups who are interested in rafting as a recreational activity, and who are seeking to explore new and exciting destinations in search of adventure and excitement.

  • Who is the typical customer for our water rafting website?
    • Answer: Our typical customer is someone who is physically fit, loves the outdoors, and is looking for a challenging and exciting adventure. They may be a solo traveler, part of a group of friends or family, or part of a team-building corporate group.
  • What are some common questions that our target audience might have before booking a trip?
    • Answer: Some common questions might include: What are the different levels of difficulty for your rafting trips? What type of gear do I need to bring? What is the cost for a trip? What is the maximum number of people allowed on each trip?
  • How can we appeal to families with children as part of our target audience?
    • Answer: To appeal to families with children, we could offer shorter, less intense rafting trips that are suitable for younger children, as well as provide family-friendly activities such as nature hikes, wildlife watching, and other outdoor activities. We could also offer discounts for families with children, as well as provide helpful information and tips for families who are new to rafting.
